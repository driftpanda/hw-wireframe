Bio

Thy Art Is Murder is an Australian deathcore band from Sydney that formed in 2006. The current lineup consists of vocalist Chris "CJ" McMahon, guitarists Sean Delander and Andy Marsh, drummer Lee Stanton and bassist Kevin Butler

Known for their punishing sound and no holds barred lyrics, Thy Art Is Murder have become a powerhouse in the metal community, spreading their message of distaste for the modern human condition.


Members

  • Cj McMahon: Vocals
  • Lee Stanton: Drums
  • Andy Marsh: Guitar
  • Sean Delander: Guitar
  • Kevin Butler: Bass
